Mission Guide

Colony Blueprint

Nexus Command (Base): Houses drones. Drop-off site for all harvested crystal cargos. Trains new Drones (Cost: 50 Crystals).

Solar Array (Power): Generates +15 Power. Essential to sustain base activities. (Cost: 150 Crystals).

Assembly Plant (Barracks): Allows training of military mechs. Requires 5 Power. Trains Raiders (Cost: 100) and Goliaths (Cost: 250).

Photon Turret (Defense): Automatic plasma weapon. Fires arcs at hostile targets within its radius. Requires 3 Power. (Cost: 175).

Exploration and Fog of War

Black regions are unexplored. Colored regions outside unit sight are under the Shroud of War (you see the landscape, but moving enemy units are hidden). Explore by sending scouts to clear the fog.
