Realm Commander

Build your settlement, gather resources, raise an army, and uncover every corner of the map.

  • Left-click Select a unit or building; drag to box-select multiple units
  • Shift-click Add to current selection
  • Right-click Move / gather / attack, depending on target
  • WASD / Arrows Scroll the camera (also scrolls near screen edges)
  • Mouse wheel Zoom in / out
  • H Center camera on Town Hall   Esc Cancel placement
  • Minimap Click or drag to jump the camera
  • Goal Grow your base and reveal the whole fog-covered map
